# WhySper

> A social network built by and for writers.

WhySper is a social platform designed for writers and writing enthusiasts. The goal is to create a receptive, community-focused space where users are more than just producers and consumers — they are active participants in shaping the quality and culture of the platform.

---

## The Reviewer System

The centerpiece of WhySper is its community-driven review system.

Every month, users vote to elect a group of **Reviewers**. Reviewers are responsible for evaluating published works across predefined criteria based on the writing style — poetry, short stories, fiction, and others. Individual scores are averaged into a final score, which directly influences the platform's algorithm, ensuring quality writing gets the visibility it deserves.

To prevent favoritism and collusion:

- Reviewer terms last **one month only**, after which a new election is held
- The community can trigger an **impeachment vote** to remove a Reviewer mid-term
- If a Reviewer is removed, another is reallocated to fill the role
